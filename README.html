Challenge

Your boss comes in and wants a splash page for the brand of a client. She says you have two hours to complete the project. You’ll present your solution in the afternoon today (5 minutes, explaining your design decisions).

Working in groups of two (chosen by mentors):

  1. Each group posts a chosen brand from Tokyo 2020 into Slack. Each group is to use a different brand - first come, first served :)
  2. Each group is to respect the brand image. Treat this like a real project.
  3. Build a splash page using content from the brand’s website. Students can choose branding style, web page design and content.
  4. Use whiteboards/butchers paper to plan your splash page.

Your HTML should validate (no errors - warnings are OK) on the W3 HTML Validator.

Your CSS should validate (no errors - warnings are OK) on the W3 CSS Validator.

You can always check the Resources section (below) for help. Also ask each other and your mentors!

Homework

  • Download the Zen Garden HTML and apply your own styles and layout to it using CSS. (You will need to do extra research and stretch yourself!)
